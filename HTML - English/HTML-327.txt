<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun_3">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=coup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=20" title="Edit section: Noun">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="fr">coup</strong>&#160;<span class="gender"><abbr title="masculine gender">m</abbr></span> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-fr p-form-of" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/coups#French" title="coups">coups</a></b>)
</p>
<ol><li><a href="/wiki/blow" title="blow">blow</a>, <a href="/wiki/hit" title="hit">hit</a>, <a href="/wiki/strike" title="strike">strike</a>
<ol><li>sound of the action
<dl><dd><i>coup de tonnerre</i></dd></dl></li>
<li>physical consequences of the action (<a href="/wiki/mark" title="mark">marks</a>)
<dl><dd><i>marqué de coups</i></dd></dl></li></ol></li>
<li>fast and instantaneous action
<dl><dd><i>jeter un <a href="/wiki/coup_d%27%C5%93il" title="coup d&#39;œil">coup d'œil</a></i></dd></dl></li>
<li>elementary action that may be repeated (e.g. in a <a href="/wiki/game" title="game">game</a>)
<dl><dd><i>boire un coup</i></dd></dl>
<ol><li>for a <a href="/wiki/firearm" title="firearm">firearm</a>, <a href="/wiki/load" title="load">load</a>
<dl><dd><i>pistolet à six coups</i></dd></dl></li></ol></li>
<li>small quantity: <i>mettre un coup de peinture</i></li>
<li>planned action
<dl><dd><i>préparer son coup</i></dd></dl></li>
<li class="mw-empty-elt"></li><li class="senseid" id="French-lay"><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#slang" title="Appendix:Glossary">slang</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> <a href="/wiki/lay" title="lay">lay</a>
<dl><dd><i>Cette meuf, c'était le meilleur <b>coup</b> de ma vie.</i></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr">un bon coup</i> ― <span class="e-translation">a good lay</span></div></dd></dl></li></ol>

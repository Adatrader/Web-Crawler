<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=mention&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Noun">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

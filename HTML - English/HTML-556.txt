<p>Participants in this <a href="/wiki/Learning_project" class="mw-redirect" title="Learning project">learning project</a> collaborate to learn about the Wikimedia Foundation. The <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://wikimediafoundation.org">Wikimedia Foundation</a> mission is to help <a href="/wiki/Wikimedia" title="Wikimedia">projects</a> like Wikiversity collect and develop educational content. What support does Wikiversity get from the Wikimedia Foundation?
</p>

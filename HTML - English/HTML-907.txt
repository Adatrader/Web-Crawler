<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Target_Audience">Target Audience</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=English_for_B2_Students&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Target Audience">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This book is intended for adult and teenage learners of English as a second language who are at B2 (Upper Intermediate) level.  B2 refers to level B2 on in the Common European Framework of Reference. Usually students of this level have studied English for at least 4 years or have previously taken the University of Cambridge PET examination or wish to sit the FCE (<i>First</i>) examination in the future.  This book is also suitable for use by students who are preparing for similar examinations, for example the 'matura' examination in Polish state schools.
</p><p>Because this book is read by people of all ages any new edits will have to be reviewed by an administrator or a reviewer before they are visible.
</p>

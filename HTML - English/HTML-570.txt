<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=melior&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>From <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proto-Italic_language" class="extiw" title="w:Proto-Italic language">Proto-Italic</a></span> <i class="Latinx mention" lang="itc-pro"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Reconstruction:Proto-Italic/melj%C5%8Ds&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Reconstruction:Proto-Italic/meljōs (page does not exist)">*meljōs</a></i>, from <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proto-Indo-European_language" class="extiw" title="w:Proto-Indo-European language">Proto-Indo-European</a></span> <i class="Latinx mention" lang="ine-pro"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/m%C3%A9ly%C5%8Ds&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/mélyōs (page does not exist)">*mélyōs</a></i>, from <i class="Latinx mention" lang="ine-pro"><a href="/wiki/Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/mel-" class="mw-redirect" title="Reconstruction:Proto-Indo-European/mel-">*mel-</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">“</span><span class="mention-gloss">strong, big</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">”</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>. Cognate with <i class="Latn mention" lang="la"><a href="/wiki/multus#Latin" title="multus">multus</a></i>, <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ancient_Greek" class="extiw" title="w:Ancient Greek">Ancient Greek</a></span> <i class="polytonic mention" lang="grc"><a href="/wiki/%CE%BC%CE%AC%CE%BB%CE%B1#Ancient_Greek" title="μάλα">μάλα</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span lang="grc-Latn" class="mention-tr tr Latn">mála</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>, <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latvian_language" class="extiw" title="w:Latvian language">Latvian</a></span> <i class="Latn mention" lang="lv"><a href="/w/index.php?title=milns&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="milns (page does not exist)">milns</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">“</span><span class="mention-gloss">very much, a lot of</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">”</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>.

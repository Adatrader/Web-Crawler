<p><b><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Florida" class="extiw" title="w:Florida">Florida</a></b> is a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/U.S._state" class="extiw" title="w:U.S. state">state</a> in the southeastern region of the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>, bordered to the west by the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gulf_of_Mexico" class="extiw" title="w:Gulf of Mexico">Gulf of Mexico</a>, to the north by <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alabama" class="extiw" title="w:Alabama">Alabama</a> and <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(U.S._state)" title="Georgia (U.S. state)">Georgia</a>, to the east by the Atlantic Ocean, and to the south by the Straits of Florida. Florida is the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_U.S._states_and_territories_by_area" class="extiw" title="w:List of U.S. states and territories by area">22nd most extensive</a>, the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_U.S._states_and_territories_by_population" class="extiw" title="w:List of U.S. states and territories by population">4th most populous</a>, and the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_U.S._states_and_territories_by_population_density" class="extiw" title="w:List of U.S. states and territories by population density">8th most densely populated</a> of the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_U.S._states" class="extiw" title="w:List of U.S. states">50 United States</a>. The state capital is <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tallahassee,_Florida" class="extiw" title="w:Tallahassee, Florida">Tallahassee</a>, the largest city is <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacksonville,_Florida" class="extiw" title="w:Jacksonville, Florida">Jacksonville</a>, and the largest metropolitan area is the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Miami_metropolitan_area" class="extiw" title="w:Miami metropolitan area">Miami metropolitan area</a>.

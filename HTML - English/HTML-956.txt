<div style="padding:.4em; text-align:left; font-weight:normal; background:#FFF; border-left: 1px solid #AAA; font-size:100%; border-right: 1px solid #AAA;">
<div class="floatright"><a href="/wiki/Fasciculus:Accueil_scribe.png" class="image"><img alt="Accueil scribe.png" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/96/Accueil_scribe.png/120px-Accueil_scribe.png" decoding="async" width="120" height="120" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/96/Accueil_scribe.png/180px-Accueil_scribe.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/96/Accueil_scribe.png/240px-Accueil_scribe.png 2x" data-file-width="1105" data-file-height="1102" /></a></div><b>Vicifons</b> est textuum quacumque lingua scriptorum thesaurus, qui aut in "dominio publico" sint aut sub <a href="https://la.wikipedia.org/wiki/Creative_Commons_licentia" class="extiw" title="w:Creative Commons licentia">Creative Commons licentiae</a> (<a href="https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/w:Creative_Commons_license" class="extiw" title="en:w:Creative Commons license">in English</a>) prolati. Situs est pars societatis <a href="https://la.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikimedia" class="extiw" title="w:Wikimedia">Vicimediae</a>, inceptus sociatus et fraternus <a href="https://la.wikipedia.org/wiki/la:Pagina_prima" class="extiw" title="w:la:Pagina prima">Vicipaediae</a>. Usque adhuc sunt in Vicifonte  <b><a href="/wiki/Specialis:Census" title="Specialis:Census">5&#160;764</a></b> paginae.<br />Si vis experimenta recensendi facere, procede ad <a href="/wiki/Vicifons:Pagina_experimentalis" title="Vicifons:Pagina experimentalis">paginam ceratam</a>.<br /><br />

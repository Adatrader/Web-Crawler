Cognate with <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ancient_Greek" class="extiw" title="w:Ancient Greek">Ancient Greek</a></span> <i class="polytonic mention" lang="grc"><a href="/wiki/%E1%BC%B5%CF%80%CF%80%CE%BF%CF%82#Ancient_Greek" title="ἵππος">ἵππος</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span lang="grc-Latn" class="mention-tr tr Latn">híppos</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>, <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sanskrit" class="extiw" title="w:Sanskrit">Sanskrit</a></span> <i class="Deva mention" lang="sa"><a href="/wiki/%E0%A4%85%E0%A4%B6%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B5#Sanskrit" title="अश्व">अश्व</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span lang="sa-Latn" class="mention-tr tr Latn">áśva</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>, <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Persian_language" class="extiw" title="w:Persian language">Persian</a></span> <i class="fa-Arab mention" lang="fa"><a href="/wiki/%D8%A7%D8%B3%D8%A8#Persian" title="اسب">اسب</a></i>&#8206; <h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=equus&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Noun">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="la">equus</strong>&#160;<span class="gender"><abbr title="masculine gender">m</abbr></span> (<i>genitive</i> <b class="Latn" lang="la"><a href="/wiki/equi#Latin" title="equi">equī</a></b>); <i><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Latin_second_declension" title="Appendix:Latin second declension">second declension</a></i>
</p>
<ol><li>a <a href="/wiki/horse" title="horse">horse</a>
<ul><li><div class="wiktQuote" data-validation="green"><b>29 <small class="ce-date"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#BCE" title="Appendix:Glossary"><span title="Glossary and display preference">BCE</span></a></small> – 19 <small class="ce-date"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#BCE" title="Appendix:Glossary"><span title="Glossary and display preference">BCE</span></a></small></b>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virgil" class="extiw" title="w:Virgil">Virgil</a>, <i><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aeneid" class="extiw" title="w:Aeneid">Aeneid</a></i> <a href="https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/la:Aeneis/Liber_II#45" class="extiw" title="s:la:Aeneis/Liber II">II.48</a>:<dl><dd><span class="Latn e-quotation" lang="la"><b>equo</b> ne credite, Teucri! Quidquid id est, timeo Danaos et dona ferentes.</span><dl><dd>Do not trust <b>the horse</b>, Trojans! Whatever it is, I fear the Danaans even if they are bearing gifts.</dd></dl></dd></dl></div></li>
<li>Vergil, Aeneis II, 48 and 110-113 and 150 (edited and translated by H. Rushton Fairclough, <i>Virgil with an English translation I</i>, 1916)
<dl><dd><b>equo</b> ne credite, Teucri.
<dl><dd>Trust not the <b>horse</b>, ye Trojans.</dd></dl></dd>
<dd>saepe illos aspera ponti | interclusit hiems et terruit Auster euntis; | praecipue, cum iam hic trabibus contextus acernis | staret <b>equus</b>, toto sonuerunt aethere nimbi.
<dl><dd>Often a fierce tempest of the deep cut them off and the gale scared them from going. Above all, when yonder <b>horse</b> now stood framed of maple-beams, storm clouds sounded throughout the sky.</dd></dl></dd>
<dd>quo molem hanc immanis <b>equi</b> statuere?
<dl><dd>To what end have they set up this huge mass of a <b>horse</b>?</dd></dl></dd></dl></li></ul></li>
<li>a <a href="/wiki/steed" title="steed">steed</a>, <a href="/wiki/charger" title="charger">charger</a>
<ul><li>Vergil, Georgicon II, 541-542 (edited and translated by H. Rushton Fairclough, <i>Virgil with an English translation I</i>, 1916)
<dl><dd>Sed nos immensum spatiis confecimus aequor, | et iam tempus equum fumantia solvere colla.
<dl><dd>But in our course we have traversed a mighty plain, and now it is time to unyoke the necks of our smoking <b>steeds</b>.</dd></dl></dd></dl></li></ul></li></ol>

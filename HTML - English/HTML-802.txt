<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Adjective">Adjective</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=grand&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Adjective">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">grand</strong> (<i><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#comparative" title="Appendix:Glossary">comparative</a></i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en comparative-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/grander#English" title="grander">grander</a></b> <i>or</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en comparative-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/more#English" title="more">more</a> grand</b>, <i><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#superlative" title="Appendix:Glossary">superlative</a></i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en superlative-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/grandest#English" title="grandest">grandest</a></b> <i>or</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en superlative-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/most#English" title="most">most</a> grand</b>)
</p>
<ol><li>Of a <a href="/wiki/large" title="large">large</a> size or extent; <a href="/wiki/great" title="great">great</a>.
<dl><dd><i>a <b>grand</b> mountain</i></dd>
<dd><i>a <b>grand</b> army</i></dd>
<dd><i>a <b>grand</b> mistake</i></dd></dl></li>
<li>Great in size, and fine or imposing in appearance or impression; <a href="/wiki/illustrious" title="illustrious">illustrious</a>, <a href="/wiki/dignified" title="dignified">dignified</a>, <a href="/wiki/magnificent" title="magnificent">magnificent</a>.
<dl><dd><i>a <b>grand</b> monarch</i></dd>
<dd><i>a <b>grand</b> view</i></dd>
<dd><i>His simple vision has transformed into something far more <b>grand</b>.</i></dd></dl>
<ul><li><div class="citation-whole"><span class="cited-source"><b>1863</b>,  Sheridan Le Fanu,  <cite>The House by the Churchyard</cite>:</span><dl><dd><div class="h-quotation"><span class="Latn e-quotation" lang="en">In the mean time, Cluffe had arrived. He was a little bit huffed and <b>grand</b> at being nailed as an evidence, upon a few words carelessly, or, if you will, confidentially dropped at his own mess-table, where Lowe chanced to be a guest; and certainly with no suspicion that his little story could in any way be made to elucidate the mystery of Sturk's murder.</span></div></dd></dl></div></li></ul></li>
<li>Having <a href="/wiki/higher" title="higher">higher</a> rank or more dignity, size, or importance than other persons or things of the same name.
<dl><dd><i>a <b>grand</b> lodge</i></dd>
<dd><i>a <b>grand</b> vizier</i></dd>
<dd><i>a <b>grand</b> piano</i></dd>
<dd><i>The Grand Viziers of the Ottoman Empire.</i></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac qualifier-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content qualifier-content">usually in compound forms</span><span class="ib-brac qualifier-brac">)</span> Standing in the second or some more remote degree of parentage or descent.
<dl><dd><i>grandfather, grandson, grand-child</i></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ireland" class="extiw" title="w:Ireland">Ireland</a><span class="ib-comma">,</span>&#32;<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northern_England" class="extiw" title="w:Northern England">Northern England</a><span class="ib-comma">,</span>&#32;<a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#colloquial" title="Appendix:Glossary">colloquial</a><span class="ib-comma">,</span>&#32;otherwise&#32;&#32;<a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#dated" title="Appendix:Glossary">dated</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> <a href="/wiki/fine" title="fine">Fine</a>; <a href="/wiki/lovely" title="lovely">lovely</a>.
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="en">A cup of tea? That'd be <b>grand</b>.</i></div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/music" title="music">music</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> Containing all the parts proper to a given form of <a href="/wiki/composition" title="composition">composition</a>.</li></ol>

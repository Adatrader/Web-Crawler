<p>This is a complete list of the <a href="/wiki/U.S._state" title="U.S. state">U.S. states</a>, <a href="/wiki/District_of_Columbia" class="mw-redirect" title="District of Columbia">federal district</a>, and its major <a href="/wiki/Territories_of_the_United_States" title="Territories of the United States">territories</a> ordered by total area, land area, and water area. The water area includes <a href="/wiki/Inland_waters" class="mw-redirect" title="Inland waters">inland waters</a>, <a href="/wiki/Coastal_waters" class="mw-redirect" title="Coastal waters">coastal waters</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lakes" title="Great Lakes">Great Lakes</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Territorial_waters" title="Territorial waters">territorial waters</a>. <a href="/wiki/Glacier" title="Glacier">Glaciers</a> and intermittent bodies of water are counted as land area.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

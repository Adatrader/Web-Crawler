<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="French">French</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=ch%C3%AAne&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: French">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=ch%C3%AAne&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>From <span class="etylcleanup"><span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_French" class="extiw" title="w:Middle French">Middle French</a></span></span> <i class="Latn mention" lang="frm"><a href="/wiki/chesne#Middle_French" title="chesne">chesne</a></i>, from <span class="etylcleanup"><span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_French" class="extiw" title="w:Old French">Old French</a></span></span> <i class="Latn mention" lang="fro"><a href="/wiki/chesne#Old_French" title="chesne">chesne</a></i>, <i class="Latn mention" lang="fro"><a href="/wiki/chaisne#Old_French" title="chaisne">chaisne</a></i>, <i class="Latn mention" lang="fro"><a href="/w/index.php?title=chasne&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="chasne (page does not exist)">chasne</a></i>, from <span class="etylcleanup"><span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vulgar_Latin" class="extiw" title="w:Vulgar Latin">Vulgar Latin</a></span></span> <i class="Latn mention" lang="la"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Reconstruction:Latin/cassanus&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Reconstruction:Latin/cassanus (page does not exist)">*cassanus</a></i> (attested in Medieval Latin as <i class="Latn mention" lang="la"><a href="/w/index.php?title=casnus&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="casnus (page does not exist)">casnus</a></i>), probably from <span class="etylcleanup"><span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaulish_language" class="extiw" title="w:Gaulish language">Gaulish</a></span></span> <i class="Latn mention" lang="cel-gau"><a href="/wiki/cassanos#Gaulish" title="cassanos">cassanos</a></i>. Some Old French forms possibly influenced by <i class="Latn mention" lang="fro"><a href="/wiki/fraisne#Old_French" title="fraisne">fraisne</a></i>, <i class="Latn mention" lang="fro"><a href="/wiki/fresne#Old_French" title="fresne">fresne</a></i> (cf. modern <i class="Latn mention" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/fr%C3%AAne#French" title="frêne">frêne</a></i>). Compare <span class="etyl"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Occitan_language" class="extiw" title="w:Occitan language">Occitan</a></span> <i class="Latn mention" lang="oc"><a href="/wiki/casse#Occitan" title="casse">casse</a></i>, Franco-Provençal <i class="Latn mention" lang="frp"><a href="/w/index.php?title=ch%C3%A2no&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="châno (page does not exist)">châno</a></i>. See also Spanish <i class="Latn mention" lang="es"><a href="/wiki/quejigo#Spanish" title="quejigo">quejigo</a></i>, Galician <i class="Latn mention" lang="gl"><a href="/wiki/caxigo#Galician" title="caxigo">caxigo</a></i>, Aragonese <i class="Latn mention" lang="an"><a href="/wiki/caixico#Aragonese" title="caixico">caixico</a></i>.
</p>

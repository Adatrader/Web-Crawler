<div class="sister-wikipedia sister-project noprint floatright" style="border: 1px solid #aaa; font-size: 90%; background: #f9f9f9; width: 250px; padding: 4px; text-align: left;"><div style="float: left;"><div class="floatnone"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/Wikipedia-logo.png/44px-Wikipedia-logo.png" decoding="async" width="44" height="44" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/Wikipedia-logo.png/66px-Wikipedia-logo.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/Wikipedia-logo.png/88px-Wikipedia-logo.png 2x" data-file-width="200" data-file-height="200" /></div></div><div style="margin-left: 60px;">English <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia" title="Wikipedia">Wikipedia</a> has an article on:<div style="margin-left: 10px;"><b class="Latn" lang="en"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/curation" class="extiw" title="w:curation">curation</a></b></div></div><span class="interProject"><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/curation" class="extiw" title="w:curation">Wikipedia </a></span></div>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Etymology">Etymology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=curation&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Etymology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Pronunciation">Pronunciation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=curation&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Pronunciation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:International_Phonetic_Alphabet" title="Wiktionary:International Phonetic Alphabet">IPA</a><sup>(<a href="/wiki/Appendix:English_pronunciation" title="Appendix:English pronunciation">key</a>)</sup>:&#32;<span class="IPA">/kjəˈɹeɪʃən/</span>, <span class="IPA">/kjʊˈɹeɪʃən/</span></li>
<li>Rhymes: <a href="/wiki/Rhymes:English/e%C9%AA%CA%83%C9%99n" title="Rhymes:English/eɪʃən"><span class="IPA">-eɪʃən</span></a></li>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=curation&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Noun">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">curation</strong> (<i><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#countable" title="Appendix:Glossary">countable</a> and <a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable" title="Appendix:Glossary">uncountable</a></i>, <i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/curations#English" title="curations">curations</a></b>)
</p>
<ol><li>The act of <a href="/wiki/curate" title="curate">curating</a>, of organizing and maintaining a collection of artworks or artifacts.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#archaic" title="Appendix:Glossary">archaic</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> The act of <a href="/wiki/cure" title="cure">curing</a> or <a href="/wiki/heal" title="heal">healing</a>.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/database" title="database">databases</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> The manual updating of information in a <a href="/wiki/database" title="database">database</a>.
<ul><li><div class="citation-whole"><span class="cited-source"><b>2009</b>,  David Edwards, Jason Stajich, David Hansen,  <cite><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://books.google.fr/books?id=Xb3IeXdtQVwC&amp;pg=PA361&amp;dq=%22Manual+database+curation+involves+the+following+steps:+%281%29+finding+articles+of+interest;+%282%29+finding+and+extracting+facts+%28relations,+events%22&amp;hl=fr&amp;sa=X&amp;ved=0CB8Q6AEwAGoVChMIqITHsLq7yAIVAioaCh2OIAbJ#v=onepage&amp;q=%22Manual%20database%20curation%20involves%20the%20following%20steps%3A%20%281%29%20finding%20articles%20of%20interest%3B%20%282%29%20finding%20and%20extracting%20facts%20%28relations%2C%20events%22&amp;f=false">Bioinformatics: Tools and Applications</a></cite>:</span><dl><dd><div class="h-quotation"><span class="Latn e-quotation" lang="en">Manual database <b>curation</b> involves the following steps: (1) finding articles of interest; (2) finding and extracting facts (relations, events, associations, etc.) relevant to the database focus; and (3) converting extracted information into predefined standardized form.</span></div></dd></dl></div></li></ul></li></ol>

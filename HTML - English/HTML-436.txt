<th width="50%">Why is this page protected?
</th>
<th>What can I do?
</th></tr>
<tr>
<td valign="top">
<ul><li>Archived Wikinews articles are fully protected, as they are considered snapshots in time, permanent records of history.</li>
<li>Published Wikinews articles are usually semi-protected from editing, and fully protected from moving, as they are exported content of the project.</li>
<li>While most general-purpose pages can be edited by anyone, sometimes page access needs to be restricted due to vandalism.</li>
<li>The reason for protection can be found in the <span class="plainlinks"><span style="color:#002bb8"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikinews.org/w/index.php?title=Special:Log&amp;type=protect&amp;page=Template:Main_around">protection log</a></span></span>. If there are no relevant entries in the protection log, the page may <span class="plainlinks"><span style="color:#002bb8"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikinews.org/w/index.php?title=Special:Log&amp;type=move&amp;page=Template:Main_around">have been moved</a></span></span> after being protected.</li></ul>
</td>
<td valign="top">
<ul><li>If you have a user account, <b><a href="/wiki/Special:UserLogin" title="Special:UserLogin">log in</a></b> first. If you do not yet have an account, you may <b><span class="plainlinks"><span style="color:#002bb8"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikinews.org/w/index.php?title=Special:UserLogin&amp;type=signup">create one</a></span></span></b>; after you've edited a bit and a few days have passed, you will be able to edit semi-protected pages.</li>
<li>If you feel an edit is needed to this page, you may wish to <b><span class="plainlinks"><span style="color:#002bb8"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikinews.org/w/index.php?title=Template_talk:Main_around&amp;action=edit&amp;section=new">start</a></span></span></b> a new thread on the talk page and use {{<a href="/wiki/Template:Editprotected" title="Template:Editprotected">editprotected</a>}} to request the edit.</li></ul>
</td></tr></tbody></table>

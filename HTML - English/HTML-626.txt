<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=avoir&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Noun">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="fr">avoir</strong>&#160;<span class="gender"><abbr title="masculine gender">m</abbr></span> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-fr p-form-of" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/avoirs#French" title="avoirs">avoirs</a></b>)
</p>
<ol><li><span class="Latn" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/asset#English" title="asset">asset</a></span>, <span class="Latn" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/possession#English" title="possession">possession</a></span></li></ol>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Verb">Verb</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=avoir&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Verb">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="fr">avoir</strong>
</p>
<ol><li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#transitive" title="Appendix:Glossary">transitive</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> to <a href="/wiki/have" title="have">have</a> <span class="gloss-brac">(</span><span class="gloss-content">to own; to possess</span><span class="gloss-brac">)</span>
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr">J'aimerais <b>avoir</b> 20 dollars.</i> ― <span class="e-translation">I would like to <b>have</b> 20 dollars.</span></div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#intransitive" title="Appendix:Glossary">intransitive</a><span class="ib-comma">,</span>&#32;with <b><a href="/wiki/%C3%A0" title="à">à</a></b></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> to <a href="/wiki/have_to" title="have to">have (to)</a>, <a href="/wiki/must" title="must">must</a>
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr">J'<b>ai</b> à vous parler.</i> ― <span class="e-translation">I <b>have</b> to talk to you.</span></div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#auxiliary" title="Appendix:Glossary">auxiliary</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> to have (auxiliary verb to form compound past tenses of most verbs)
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr">J'<b>ai</b> parlé.</i> ― <span class="e-translation">I <b>have</b> spoken.</span></div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#transitive" title="Appendix:Glossary">transitive</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> to have <span class="gloss-brac">(</span><span class="gloss-content">a condition</span><span class="gloss-brac">)</span>
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr">J'<b>ai</b> faim.</i> ― <span class="e-translation">I'm hungry.</span> (literally, “<span class="e-literally">I have hunger.</span>”)</div></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr">J'<b>ai</b> froid.</i> ― <span class="e-translation">I'm cold.</span> (literally, “<span class="e-literally">I have cold.</span>”)</div></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#transitive" title="Appendix:Glossary">transitive</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> to have (a <a href="/wiki/measure" title="measure">measure</a> or <a href="/wiki/age" title="age">age</a>)
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr">Elle <b>a</b> 19 ans.</i> ― <span class="e-translation">She is 19 years old.</span> (literally, “<span class="e-literally">She has 19 years.</span>”)</div></dd></dl></li>
<li>to <a href="/wiki/have" title="have">have</a> <span class="gloss-brac">(</span><span class="gloss-content">to trick</span><span class="gloss-brac">)</span>
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr">Tu t'es fait <b>avoir</b>.</i> ― <span class="e-translation">You've been <b>had</b>.</span></div></dd></dl></li>
<li>to <a href="/wiki/have" title="have">have</a> (to participate in an experience)
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr"><b>avoir</b> des relations sexuelles</i><dl><dd><span class="e-translation"><b>to have</b> sexual relations</span></dd></dl></div></dd></dl></li></ol>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Usage_notes">Usage notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=avoir&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Usage notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><i class="Latn mention" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/avoir#French" title="avoir">Avoir</a></i> is often used with nouns like <i class="Latn mention" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/chaud#French" title="chaud">chaud</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">“</span><span class="mention-gloss">heat</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">”</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>, <i class="Latn mention" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/faim#French" title="faim">faim</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">“</span><span class="mention-gloss">hunger</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">”</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>, <i class="Latn mention" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/soif#French" title="soif">soif</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">“</span><span class="mention-gloss">thirst</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">”</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>, <i class="Latn mention" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/peur#French" title="peur">peur</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">“</span><span class="mention-gloss">fear</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">”</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span>, etc. to express a personal condition or feeling, as shown in examples above. While constructions like <i class="Latn mention" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/%C3%AAtre#French" title="être">être</a> <a href="/wiki/affam%C3%A9#French" title="affamé">affamé</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">“</span><span class="mention-gloss">to be starving/starved</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">”</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span> and  <i class="Latn mention" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/%C3%AAtre#French" title="être">être</a> <a href="/wiki/assoiff%C3%A9#French" title="assoiffé">assoiffé</a></i> <span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">(</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">“</span><span class="mention-gloss">to be thirsty</span><span class="mention-gloss-double-quote">”</span><span class="mention-gloss-paren annotation-paren">)</span> exist, they are almost always used figuratively. It is always more natural to use <i class="Latn mention" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/avoir#French" title="avoir">avoir</a></i> rather than <i class="Latn mention" lang="fr"><a href="/wiki/%C3%AAtre#French" title="être">être</a></i> in the examples listed above, and other similar cases. In some cases, both verbs can be used, but with vastly different meanings:
</p>
<dl><dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr">J'ai chaud.</i> ― <span class="e-translation">I'm hot. (I feel hot)</span> (literally, “<span class="e-literally">I have the heat.</span>”)</div></dd>
<dd><div class="h-usage-example"><i class="Latn mention e-example" lang="fr">Je suis chaud.</i> ― <span class="e-translation">I'm horny.</span></div></dd></dl>

<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="fro">avoir</strong>&#160;<span class="gender"><abbr title="masculine gender">m</abbr></span> (<i>oblique plural</i> <b class="Latn" lang="fro"><a href="/wiki/avoirs#Old_French" title="avoirs">avoirs</a></b>, <i>nominative singular</i> <b class="Latn" lang="fro"><a href="/wiki/avoirs#Old_French" title="avoirs">avoirs</a></b>, <i>nominative plural</i> <b class="Latn" lang="fro"><strong class="selflink">avoir</strong></b>)

<ul><li><b>circa 1170</b>, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chr%C3%A9tien_de_Troyes" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Chrétien de Troyes">Chrétien de Troyes</a>, <i><a href="https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/fr:%C3%89rec_et_%C3%89nide" class="extiw" title="s:fr:Érec et Énide">Érec et Énide</a></i>:

<dl><dd>It is my possession, it is my treasure.</dd></dl></dd></dl></li>
<li><div class="citation-whole"><span class="cited-source"><b>circa 1250</b>,  <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rutebeuf" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Rutebeuf">Rutebeuf</a>,  <cite><a href="https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/fr:Ci_encoumence_la_vie_de_Sainte_Marie_l%27Egypcienne" class="extiw" title="s:fr:Ci encoumence la vie de Sainte Marie l&#39;Egypcienne">Ci encoumence la vie de Sainte Marie l'Egypcienne</a></cite>:</span><dl><dd><div class="h-quotation"><span class="Latn e-quotation" lang="fro">Robes, deniers ne autre <b>avoir</b><br />Ne voloit de l'autrui avoir.</span><dl><dd><span class="e-translation">Not clothing, nor money, nor other possessions<br />Did she want to have from others.</span></dd></dl></div></dd></dl></div></li></ul></li></ol>

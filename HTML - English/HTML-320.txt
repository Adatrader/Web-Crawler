<p><b>National Guard</b> is the name used by a wide variety of current and historical uniformed organizations in different countries. The original <a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(France)" title="National Guard (France)">National Guard</a> was formed during the <a href="/wiki/French_Revolution" title="French Revolution">French Revolution</a> around a cadre of defectors from the <a href="/wiki/Gardes_Fran%C3%A7aises" title="Gardes Françaises">French Guards</a>.
</p><p><b>National Guard</b> may refer to:

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Africa">Africa</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=National_Guard&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Africa">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(Egypt)" class="mw-redirect" title="National Guard (Egypt)">National Guard (Egypt)</a>, a paramilitary force under the command of the Ministry of Defense</li>
<li><a href="/w/index.php?title=National_Guard_(Guinea-Bissau)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="National Guard (Guinea-Bissau) (page does not exist)">National Guard (Guinea-Bissau)</a>, a gendarmerie force of <a href="/wiki/Guinea-Bissau" title="Guinea-Bissau">Guinea-Bissau</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(Mauritania)" title="National Guard (Mauritania)">National Guard (Mauritania)</a>, the National Guard of Mauritania</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Tunisian_National_Guard" title="Tunisian National Guard">Tunisian National Guard</a>, a separate military force of Tunisia</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Americas">Americas</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=National_Guard&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Americas">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(Brazil)" class="mw-redirect" title="National Guard (Brazil)">National Guard (Brazil)</a>, a paramilitary militia created in 1831 to support the Brazilian Army</li>
<li><a href="/w/index.php?title=National_Guard_(Dominican_Republic)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="National Guard (Dominican Republic) (page does not exist)">National Guard (Dominican Republic)</a> (Guardia Nacional), a military force; see <a href="/wiki/History_of_the_Dominican_Republic" title="History of the Dominican Republic">History of the Dominican Republic</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(El_Salvador)" title="National Guard (El Salvador)">National Guard (El Salvador)</a> (1912–1992), the Salvadoran gendarmerie</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(Mexico)" title="National Guard (Mexico)">National Guard (Mexico)</a>, a gendarmerie created in 2019.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(Nicaragua)" title="National Guard (Nicaragua)">National Guard (Nicaragua)</a> (1925–1979), a militia and a gendarmerie created during the occupation by the United States</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/United_States_National_Guard" title="United States National Guard">United States National Guard</a>, military reserves organized by each of the 50 U.S. states, territories, and D.C.
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Army_National_Guard" title="Army National Guard">Army National Guard</a>, a reserve force of the United States Army which functions as the ground component of the state-level militia while not in federal service</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Air_National_Guard" title="Air National Guard">Air National Guard</a>, a reserve force of the United States Air Force which functions as the air component of the state-level militia while not in federal service</li></ul></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Venezuelan_National_Guard" title="Venezuelan National Guard">Venezuelan National Guard</a> (Guardia Nacional de Venezuela), one of the four components of the National Armed Forces of Venezuela</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">Asia</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=National_Guard&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Asia">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Azerbaijani_National_Guard" title="Azerbaijani National Guard">Azerbaijani National Guard</a>, an armed force of the Government of Azerbaijan, and operates as a semi-independent entity</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(Bahrain)" title="National Guard (Bahrain)">National Guard (Bahrain)</a>, a separate military force in Bahrain that serves both as defence force and as a security force</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Security_Guard" title="National Security Guard">National Security Guard</a>, a special force in <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a> primarily utilised for counter-terrorism activities</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(Iraq)" title="National Guard (Iraq)">National Guard (Iraq)</a>, part of the new Military of Iraq absorbed by the Iraqi Army</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_of_Kazakhstan" title="National Guard of Kazakhstan">National Guard of Kazakhstan</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(Kyrgyzstan)" title="National Guard (Kyrgyzstan)">National Guard (Kyrgyzstan)</a>, One of the regiments of the <a href="/wiki/Armed_Forces_of_the_Republic_of_Kyrgyzstan" class="mw-redirect" title="Armed Forces of the Republic of Kyrgyzstan">Armed Forces of the Republic of Kyrgyzstan</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kuwait_National_Guard" title="Kuwait National Guard">Kuwait National Guard</a>, an internal and border security force part of Kuwait Military Forces</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_of_Pakistan" title="National Guard of Pakistan">National Guard of Pakistan</a>, a paramilitary force mainly charged with air defense</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Saudi_Arabian_National_Guard" title="Saudi Arabian National Guard">Saudi Arabian National Guard</a>, one of the three major branches of the Armed Forces of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sri_Lanka_National_Guard" title="Sri Lanka National Guard">Sri Lanka National Guard</a>, the largest regiment in the Sri Lanka Army</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Presidential_National_Guard" class="mw-redirect" title="Presidential National Guard">Presidential National Guard</a>, a special operations branch of the <a href="/wiki/Military_of_Tajikistan" class="mw-redirect" title="Military of Tajikistan">Tajik Armed Forces</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Turkmen_National_Guard" title="Turkmen National Guard">Turkmen National Guard</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Uzbekistan_National_Guard" title="Uzbekistan National Guard">Uzbekistan National Guard</a></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Europe">Europe</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=National_Guard&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Europe">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/National_Guards_Unit_of_Bulgaria" title="National Guards Unit of Bulgaria">National Guards Unit of Bulgaria</a>, successor of the Personal Cavalry Convoy of Knyaz Alexander I, re-established in 2001</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Cypriot_National_Guard" title="Cypriot National Guard">Cypriot National Guard</a>, combined arms military force of the Republic of Cyprus</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Estonian_Defence_League" title="Estonian Defence League">Estonian Defence League</a>, unified paramilitary armed forces of the Republic of Estonia</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/White_Guard_(Finland)" title="White Guard (Finland)">White Guard (Finland)</a> (1918–1944), voluntary militia of the Whites in the Finnish Civil War of 1918</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(France)" title="National Guard (France)">National Guard (France)</a>, militias formed in each city from the time of the French Revolution to the Paris Commune, re-established after several terrorist attacks in 2016</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_of_Georgia" title="National Guard of Georgia">National Guard of Georgia</a>, a military structure within the Georgian Armed Forces</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(Greece)" title="National Guard (Greece)">National Guard (Greece)</a>, a military structure within the <a href="/wiki/Hellenic_Army" title="Hellenic Army">Hellenic Army</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_(Ireland)" class="mw-redirect" title="National Guard (Ireland)">National Guard (Ireland)</a>, better known as the Blueshirts, a 1930s political movement in Ireland</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Republican_Guard_(Italy)" title="National Republican Guard (Italy)">National Republican Guard (Italy)</a>, a paramilitary force of the Italian Social Republic</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Latvian_National_Guard" title="Latvian National Guard">Latvian National Guard</a>, a volunteer paramilitary armed force, part of National Armed Forces of Latvia</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Republican_Guard_(Portugal)" title="National Republican Guard (Portugal)">National Republican Guard (Portugal)</a>, the gendarmerie of Portugal</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_of_Russia" title="National Guard of Russia">National Guard of Russia</a>, gendarmerie force of Russia, formed in 2016</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Militia_(Spain)" title="National Militia (Spain)">National Militia (Spain)</a>, 19th century quasi-military force comparable to the National Guard in France</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/National_Guard_of_Ukraine" title="National Guard of Ukraine">National Guard of Ukraine</a>, formed 1991–2000 and 2014–current</li></ul>

<p>I am a <a href="/wiki/Wikiversity:Custodianship" title="Wikiversity:Custodianship">custodian</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wikiversity:Bureaucrat" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikiversity:Bureaucrat">bureaucrat</a> on <a href="/wiki/Wikiversity" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikiversity">English Wikiversity</a> since mid-2008. Feel free to help me - or help me to help you&#160;:).<br />
I strive to be an "<a href="/wiki/Open_academic" class="mw-redirect" title="Open academic">open academic</a>" - i.e., to make my <a href="/wiki/User:Jtneill/Teaching" title="User:Jtneill/Teaching">teaching</a>, <a href="/wiki/User:Jtneill/Research" title="User:Jtneill/Research">research</a> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=User:Jtneill/Service&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="User:Jtneill/Service (page does not exist)">other professional activities</a> free and open to all. Read more about my <a href="/wiki/User:Jtneill/Teaching/Technologies/Personal_story" title="User:Jtneill/Teaching/Technologies/Personal story">personal story</a>. My best OER contributions on WV have been <a href="/wiki/Social_psychology_(psychology)" title="Social psychology (psychology)">Social psychology (psychology)</a> and <a href="/wiki/Motivation_and_emotion" title="Motivation and emotion">Motivation and emotion</a>. Here's my <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://toolserver.org/~river/cgi-bin/count_edits?user=jtneill&amp;dbname=enwikiversity_p">edit profile</a>. I work as an Assistant Professor in the Centre for Applied Psychology, <a href="/wiki/University_of_Canberra" title="University of Canberra">University of Canberra</a>.

<p>Welcome to the <b>English Language Division</b> of the Schools of <a href="/wiki/School:Language_and_Literature" class="mw-redirect" title="School:Language and Literature">Language and Literature</a> and <a href="/wiki/School:Linguistics" class="mw-redirect" title="School:Linguistics">Linguistics</a>.
</p>
<dl><dd><i>For studies of English Literature, please see <a href="/wiki/Topic:Literary_Studies" class="mw-redirect" title="Topic:Literary Studies">Literary Studies</a>.</i></dd></dl>
<p>The English Language Division coordinates and focuses studies on the English language which relate to the language itself, and not its literary tradition.  Studies and projects on Old and Middle English, as well as English grammar and linguistics will be listed here.
</p>

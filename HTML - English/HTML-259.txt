<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun_2">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=strand&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Noun">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">strand</strong> (<i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/strands#English" title="strands">strands</a></b>)
</p>
<ol><li>Each of the strings which, twisted together, make up a <a href="/wiki/yarn" title="yarn">yarn</a>, <a href="/wiki/rope" title="rope">rope</a> or <a href="/wiki/cord" title="cord">cord</a>.</li>
<li>A <a href="/wiki/string" title="string">string</a>.</li>
<li>An <a href="/wiki/individual" title="individual">individual</a> <a href="/wiki/length" title="length">length</a> of any fine, <a href="/wiki/string" title="string">string</a>-like substance.
<dl><dd><i><b>strand</b> of spaghetti</i></dd>
<dd><i><b>strand</b> of hair</i>.</dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/electronics" title="electronics">electronics</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A group of wires, usually twisted or braided.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/broadcasting#Noun" title="broadcasting">broadcasting</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A series of programmes on a particular <a href="/wiki/theme" title="theme">theme</a> or linked subject.</li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#figurative" title="Appendix:Glossary">figuratively</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> An <a href="/wiki/element" title="element">element</a> in a composite whole; a sequence of linked events or facts; a <a href="/wiki/logical" title="logical">logical</a> <a href="/wiki/thread" title="thread">thread</a>.
<dl><dd><i><b>strand</b> of truth</i></dd></dl>
<ul><li><b>2004</b>, David Wray, <i>Literacy: Major Themes in Education</i>, Taylor &amp; Francis <a href="/wiki/Special:BookSources/9780415277129" title="Special:BookSources/9780415277129">→ISBN</a>, page 78

<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/genetics" title="genetics">genetics</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> A nucleotide chain.</li></ol>

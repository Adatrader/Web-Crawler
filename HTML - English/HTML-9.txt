		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>Welcome to the Wikibooks Community Portal. You can find all sorts of useful links here, regardless of whether you want to read, edit, write or participate in other ways. You can play around in the <a href="/wiki/Wikibooks:Sandbox" title="Wikibooks:Sandbox">sandbox</a> to get a feel for how the <a href="/wiki/Help:Editing" title="Help:Editing">editing system</a> works. If you <a href="/wiki/Wikibooks:LOGIN" class="mw-redirect" title="Wikibooks:LOGIN">create an account</a>, you can track your changes and more. You can ask questions or start a discussion in the <a href="/wiki/Wikibooks:Reading_room" title="Wikibooks:Reading room">reading room</a>. You can also <a href="/wiki/Wikibooks:Requested_books" title="Wikibooks:Requested books">request new books</a> or start one yourself.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Noun">Noun</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=copyright&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Noun">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><strong class="Latn headword" lang="en">copyright</strong> (<i>usually <a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable" title="Appendix:Glossary">uncountable</a></i>, <i>plural</i> <b class="Latn form-of lang-en p-form-of" lang="en"><a href="/wiki/copyrights#English" title="copyrights">copyrights</a></b>)
</p>
<ol><li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#uncountable" title="Appendix:Glossary">uncountable</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> The right by law to be the entity which determines who may publish, copy and distribute a piece of writing, music, picture or other work of authorship.
<dl><dd><i><b>Copyright</b> is a separate legal area from trademarks.</i></dd></dl></li>
<li><span class="ib-brac">(</span><span class="ib-content"><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Glossary#countable" title="Appendix:Glossary">countable</a></span><span class="ib-brac">)</span> Such an exclusive right as it pertains to one or more specific works.
<dl><dd><i>The artist lost the <b>copyrights</b> to her songs when she signed the contract.</i></dd></dl></li></ol>

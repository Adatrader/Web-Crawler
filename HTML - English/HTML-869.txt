<p><b><a href="/wiki/Quotation" title="Quotation">Quotation</a></b> is the repetition of someone else's statement or thoughts. <a href="/wiki/Quotation_mark" title="Quotation mark">Quotation marks</a> are punctuation marks used in text to indicate a quotation. Both of these words are sometimes abbreviated as "quote(s)".
</p>

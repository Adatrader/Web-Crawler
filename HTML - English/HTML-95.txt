<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Contact_and_discussions">Contact and discussions</span></h2>
<p>Contact, discussions and requests on Wiktionary:
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Discussion_rooms" title="Wiktionary:Discussion rooms">Discussion rooms</a> or forums:
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Information_desk" title="Wiktionary:Information desk">Information desk</a>&#160;– for questions from <i>newcomers</i> and <i>newbies</i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Tea_room" title="Wiktionary:Tea room">Tea room</a>&#160;– for questions concerning particular words</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Etymology_scriptorium" title="Wiktionary:Etymology scriptorium">Etymology scriptorium</a>&#160;– for discussion of word origin</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Beer_parlour" title="Wiktionary:Beer parlour">Beer parlour</a>&#160;– for policy discussion and cross-entry discussion</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Grease_pit" title="Wiktionary:Grease pit">Grease pit</a>&#160;– for technical discussion such as on templates, wiki dumps and JavaScript tweaks</li></ul></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Request_pages" title="Wiktionary:Request pages">Request pages</a>&#160;– for processes such as attestation and deletion
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Requests_for_verification" title="Wiktionary:Requests for verification">Requests for verification</a>&#160;– requests for attestation of entries by providing citations
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Requests_for_verification/English" title="Wiktionary:Requests for verification/English">Requests for verification/English</a>&#160;– requests for attestation of English entries</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Requests_for_verification/Non-English" title="Wiktionary:Requests for verification/Non-English">Requests for verification/Non-English</a>&#160;– requests for attestation of foreign entries</li></ul></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Requests_for_deletion" title="Wiktionary:Requests for deletion">Requests for deletion</a>&#160;– requests for deletion for reasons not related to a lack of appropriate citations
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Requests_for_deletion/English" title="Wiktionary:Requests for deletion/English">Requests for deletion/English</a>&#160;– requests for deletion of English entries</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Requests_for_deletion/Non-English" title="Wiktionary:Requests for deletion/Non-English">Requests for deletion/Non-English</a>&#160;– requests for deletion of foreign entries</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Requests_for_deletion/Others" title="Wiktionary:Requests for deletion/Others">Requests for deletion/Others</a>&#160;– requests for deletion of pages such as categories, appendices and templates</li></ul></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Requests_for_moves,_mergers_and_splits" title="Wiktionary:Requests for moves, mergers and splits">Requests for moves, mergers and splits</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Requests_for_cleanup" title="Wiktionary:Requests for cleanup">Requests for cleanup</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Translation_requests" title="Wiktionary:Translation requests">Translation requests</a></li></ul></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Votes" title="Wiktionary:Votes">Votes</a>&#160;– votes that establish community policies</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Category:Wiktionary_fun_stuff" title="Category:Wiktionary fun stuff">Fun stuff</a>&#160;– fun community competitions</li></ul>
<p>Other means of communication:
</p>
<ul><li>Sign up to our <a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Mailing_lists" title="Wiktionary:Mailing lists">mailing list</a></li>
<li>Chat with <a href="/wiki/Wiktionarian" title="Wiktionarian">Wiktionarians</a> in <a href="/wiki/real-time" title="real-time">real-time</a> on <a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:IRC_channel" title="Wiktionary:IRC channel">our IRC channel</a> or on <a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Discord_server" title="Wiktionary:Discord server">our Discord server</a>.</li>
<li>Get help from the <a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Administrators" title="Wiktionary:Administrators">administrators</a>.</li>
<li>Find out more about the <a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Wiktionarians" title="Wiktionary:Wiktionarians">Wiktionarians</a>.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Projects">Projects</span></h2>
<p>There are a number of Wiktionary projects on the go at any one time, developing certain aspects of Wiktionary. All contributions are welcome.
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary_talk:Phrasebook" title="Wiktionary talk:Phrasebook">Phrasebook</a>&#160;– a multilingual resource for learning simple phrases in foreign languages</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Appendix:Swadesh_lists" title="Appendix:Swadesh lists">Swadesh lists</a>&#160;– lists of approximately 200 basic vocabulary words in various world languages</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Thesaurus" title="Wiktionary:Thesaurus">Thesaurus</a>&#160;– a Wiki-based <a href="/wiki/thesaurus" title="thesaurus">thesaurus</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Concordances" title="Wiktionary:Concordances">Concordances</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Frequency_lists" title="Wiktionary:Frequency lists">Frequency lists</a>&#160;– lists of the most common words from various sources</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Index_to_templates" class="mw-redirect" title="Wiktionary:Index to templates">Index to templates</a>&#160;– trying to establish a master list of <a href="/wiki/template" title="template">templates</a> in use in Wiktionary, to avoid errors in creating new templates unnecessarily. Please add any templates you know to this list.
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Wiktionary:Inflection_templates" class="mw-redirect" title="Wiktionary:Inflection templates">Inflection templates</a></li></ul></li></ul>

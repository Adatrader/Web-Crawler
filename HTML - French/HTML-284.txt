<p>The <i>Wikinews</i> Print Edition is an edition of <i>Wikinews</i> designed to offer a different way of reading Wikinews. It is also designed to help bring new users in, by allowing people to print copies and distribute them to those who would never otherwise hear about <i>Wikinews</i>. 
</p><p>Production of the print edition has been variable over the years. Below can be found an archive of previous issues.
</p><p>You can leave feedback about the print edition <span class="plainlinks"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikinews.org/w/index.php?title=Wikinews_talk:Print_edition&amp;action=edit&amp;section=new">here</a></span>.
</p>
